03 · Metrics to track

Test, test, test.

Once we have history on these metrics, we can read patterns and find what to test next to lift views and retention. A/B tests apply to everything in content, and YouTube Analytics gives the detailed data to run them properly.

Write the numbers in Notion every Monday. If a metric drops two weeks in a row, that's a signal worth investigating.

The retention valley · interactive

Hover along the curve.

A valley reveals 2 scenarios: viewers skipped the segment (the obvious read) — or, right before the valley, there's a moment they rewatched several times. That high-value moment becomes a Short / hook that funnels back into the full video.
